Symptoms

Symptoms can vary widely or not present at all. Some patients may have abnormal bumps, enigmatic fevers ,night sweats, or experience unintentional weight loss.

Prevention

This includes maintaining a healthy lifestyle, avoiding exposure to known carcinogens, taking vaccines, and pursuing periodic check-ups, and eating healthy food.

Causes

Common causes include smoking, poor diet, insufficient physical activity, exposure to sunlight, radiation, viruses ,and infections, which are prevalent contributing factors.
